Hardware

What are the dimensions of the air sensor?
2.95" x 0.77" or in metric 75mm x 19.5mm (diameter x height)
Which parameters does the air sensor measure?
Temperature, Humidity, VPD and Light (Visible/IR)
How long is the USB cord and hanging kit?
The USB cord is 15 feet and the hanging kit is 10 feet
How many should I have in my grow space?
Grow spaces have different microclimates. Typically we recommend 1-2 sensors per 25 square feet. The bigger your grow space, the more sensors you’ll need. Higher air flow means fewer sensors. Taller spaces or racks, on the other hand, means you need more sensors to have accurate data. In terms of hardware limitations, it's probably best to limit your router to around 50 units before you will see any performance issues.
How often are measurements taken by the sensor?
Parameter readings are uploaded every 15 minutes automatically. If you'd like a real-time measurement simply press the right button on the sensor. You'll see a red flash followed by some green and white blinking. Check your app momentarily to see the readings.

Privacy

What data do you store?
We collect and store your parameter data in order to show it to you. We will never share this information with anybody without your consent. Eventually, we have ambitions to anonymize the data in order to share it with the community so that everyone can learn more and compare their own parameters to other people growing the same crops.
Where is my data stored?
We store your data in our secure and private AWS cloud. We always follow all best practices around the storing of information.

Frequently Asked Questions

If you have some other questions please contact support@mendelsensors.com.


Hardware

What are the dimensions of the air sensor?
2.95" x 0.77" or in metric 75mm x 19.5mm (diameter x height)
Which parameters does the air sensor measure?
Temperature, Humidity, VPD and Lux
How long is the USB cord and hanging kit?
The USB cord is 15 feet and the hanging kit is 10 feet
How many should I have in my grow space?
Grow spaces have different microclimates. Typically we recommend 1-2 sensors per 25 square feet. The bigger your grow space, the more sensors you’ll need. Higher air flow means fewer sensors. Taller spaces or racks, on the other hand, means you need more sensors to have accurate data. In terms of hardware limitations, it's probably best to limit your router to around 50 units before you will see any performance issues.
How often are measurements taken by the sensor?
Parameters are read every 2 minutes and uploaded every 10 mins or in real time if you use the app. If any of your parameters have changed significantly since the previous reading or passed user-set thresholds we will upload the measurements before the ten minute baseline in order to still be able to alert you on a 2 minute by 2 minute basis.

Software

What happens if the air sensors loses wifi connectivity?
As long as the sensor has power it will continue to record data up to 7 days and will upload the results once it re-connects to wifi. After 30 minutes of not hearing from your sensor, we will send a notification alerting you to the fact that it has either lost WiFi or power. You will not receive any alerts about parameters being above/below thresholds however until the sensor is reconnected to the internet.
How many sensors can I have?
How many routers you got? We recommend a max of 50 sensors per WiFi router for optimal service. If you need more than 10+ sensor we recommend giving us a call so that we can work with you for the optimal setup.

Only as often as you’d like. You are in control of which alerts, if any, you receive. You can also send alerts to your email if you prefer not to receive alerts on your phone.

Privacy

What data do you store?
We collect and store your parameter data in order to show it to you. We will never share this information with anybody without your consent. Eventually, we have ambitions to anonymize the data in order to share it with the community so that everyone can learn more and compare their own parameters to other people growing the same crops.
Where is my data stored?
We store your data in our secure and private AWS cloud. We always follow all best practices around the storing of information.

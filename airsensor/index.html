How many sensors do I need?

Grow spaces can be thought of as having numerous micro climates. The bigger your space, the more sensors you will need to have the most accurate data collection.

Grow Tents/Hobby Growers

“For small areas or tents up to a 4'x8' you should be using 1-3 sensors. You should consider factors like air flow (higher flow means less sensors) and tent height (taller tent means more sensors).”

Grow Rooms

For medium sized grow rooms we typically recommend 1-2 sensors per 25 square feet. You'll start to have more micro climates within your space.

Commercial Facility/Greenhouse

Every facility is unique and uses different technology that affects its micro climates, you should contact us for a tailored response.
